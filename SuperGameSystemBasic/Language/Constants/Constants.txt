Constants
 
Constants are values that cannot be altered by the program during normal excutions.
Generally constants are pre-fixed wiht a '@' with the exception of TRUE wich is 1 and FALSE being 0.
 
Constants in the SGS are broken up into
COLORS the Custom SGS pallete
GLYPHS the 192 visiably representable console character
KEYS the extended key range
SOUNDS Sound Signal yype constants
OTHER special and or usefull constants.
