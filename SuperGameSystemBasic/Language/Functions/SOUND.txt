SOUND : SOUND(frequency,amplitude,duration.....)
Plays a sound with the specific frequency amplitude and duration in milliseconds.
example 1
SOUND(550,16384,500) 
plays a sound at 550hz at the maximum amplitide of 16384 for 500ms
example 2
SOUND(550,16384,0,500,600,16384,500,1) 
plays a sound at 550hz at the maximum amplitide of 16384 for 500ms using a sine tone then 
a sound at 600hz at the maximum amplitide of 16384 for 500ms using the sawtooth tone
